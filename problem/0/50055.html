50055. Waiting Time at Supermarket

I'm a slow walker, but I never walk backwards.

  • Status
  • Statistics

Task Description

Write a program to compute the total waiting time of customers in a supermarket when they check out.

There are MM customers shopping in a supermarket, and each of them, mimi, will be ready to check out at time titi, and it takes time sisi for him/her to check out. However, there are only NN service counters, and each of them can only serve one customer at a time. If a customer is ready to check out but all service counters are busy, then he/she will wait in a queue, until a service counter becomes available. The customers will wait in the queue in a first-come-first-serve order. Your task is to compute the total waiting time of all customers.

For example, if there are two service counters c1c1 and c2c2, four customers arriving at time 00, 55, 1010, 1515, and will spend time 2020, 2020, 2020, 2020 during checkout, respectively. The first customer m1m1 can be served immediately at c1c1 without waiting, and the second customer m2m2 can also be served immediately at c2c2 without waiting. However, when m3m3 arrives at time 1010, he has to wait in the queue because all the counters are busy, and so does m4m4 when he arrives at time 1515. After m1m1 finishes at time 2020, the first customer in a queue, who is m3m3, could finally be served. As a result m3m3 waits in the queue for 1010 seconds, and will be served at time 2020 at c1c1. Similarly customer m4m4 will be served at time 2525 at counter c2c2, after he waits in the queue for 1010 seconds. The total waiting time of all customers is therefore 2020 seconds.

Hint

You only need to maintain the ready time of all counters, and always go to the earliest available counter for checking out.

Input Format

Input contains one test case. The first line contains two integers NN and MM, indicating the number of the service counters and the number of the customers.

For the following MM lines, each line contains two integers titi and sisi, indicating the ithith customer arrives at time titi, and had to spend sisi seconds while checking out.

Note that the MM lines will be ordered by arriving time tt ascending, and if multiple customers have the same arriving time, then their order in waiting queue should be the same as the input order.

Technical limitation

  • 1≤N≤10241≤N≤1024
  • 0≤M≤1060≤M≤106
  • 0≤ti≤1040≤ti≤104
  • 0≤si≤1020≤si≤102

Output Format

Output one line with one integer indicating the total waiting time.

Sample Input 1

2 4
0 20
5 20
10 20
15 20

Sample Output 1

20

Sample Input 2

1 3
0 20
0 20
0 20

Sample Output 2

60
