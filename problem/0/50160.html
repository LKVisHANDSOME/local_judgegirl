50160. Skills

I'm a slow walker, but I never walk backwards.

  • Status
  • Statistics

Task Description

We will kill dragons with attack skills. We have four skills to attck the dragon, including three special attacks AA, BB, CC, and one normal attack. A normal attack has a base damage. Each special attack has a base damage bb, plus extra damage ee, and a cooldown cc.

We first describe how to play this game. For each round from 0 to R−1R−1, we choose the skill that will cause the maximum damage to attack the dragon. We can use a noraml attack at every round and cause its base damage, but not for special skills since they have cool downs. That is, there are two cases for when we can use a special attack. For the first special attack of a particular kind, we can use it at any round. For the second time and after, we can use a special attack at round rr only if r−r′r−r′ is at least its cool down cc, where r′r′ is the last round we used that special attack.

The damage calculation is a little bit complicated for special attacks. If the player uses a special attack at round rr for the first time, there are two cases. If r≤cr≤c, the skill causes base damage bb. If r>cr>c, the skill causes b+e(r−c)b+e(r−c) damage. However, if the player uses a special attack at round rr for the second time or later, the damage is b+e(r−r′−c)b+e(r−r′−c), where r′r′ is the last time that special attack was used. Note that (r−r′−c)(r−r′−c) is always non-negative since this is necessary for using the special attack.

The game will go for at most RR rounds. First you have to choose a skill that causes the maximum damage to the dragon. If there are more than one skill having the maximum damage, choose one in the order of AA, BB, CC, then the normal attack. Second, subtract the damage from the dragon’s health. If the dragon’s health is still positive, the dragon lives and we add gg to its health, to the maximum of HH. That is, if the health exceeds HH, then it becomes HH. On the other hand, if the health becomes 0 or negative after the attack, the dragon dies and we report that the game ends.
You need to output the health of the dragon after the damage and before adding gg at each round.

Let us explain the process by an example. We assume that H=20H=20, g=1g=1, and R=30R=30. Also the information of the attack skills are listed below.

base damage extra damage cool down
special attack A 4 1 2
special attack B 3 1 2
special attack C 2 1 2
normal attack N 1 X X

When rr is 0, attack AA causes damage 4, attack BB causes 3, attack CC causes 2, and the normal attack causes 1, so we choose A. The dragon’s health becomes 20−4=1620−4=16 so you output 16. After that, the dragon will regent 1 health and it becomes 17.

When rr is 1, attack AA is in cool down and can be used again at or after r=2r=2. Attack BB has damage 3, attack CC has 2, and the normal attack has 1, so we choose BB. The dragon’s health becomes 17−3=1417−3=14, and we output 14. After that, the dragon will regent 1 health and it becomes 15.

When rr is 2, attack BB is in cool down. Attack AA can be used again,and its damage is 4. Attack CC has damage 2, and the normal attack has damage 1, so we choose AA. The dragon’s health becomes 15−4=1115−4=11 and we output 11. Then the dragon will regent 1 health and it becomes 12.

When rr is 3, attack AA is in cool down, attack BB can be used again and it has damage 3. Attack CC has damage 2+1∗(3−2)=32+1∗(3−2)=3 because it has extra damage at this round. Attack B and C has the same damage, so we choose B according to the priority. The dragon's health becomes 12−3=912−3=9, and we output 9. Then the dragon will regent 1 health and it becomes 10.

The game will repeat for RR rounds or dragon’s health becomes 0 or negative during the game.

Input Format

There will be 6 lines in the inputs. The first line is a positive integer RR. The second line contains two integers, HH and gg, which are dragon's maximum health points and the health regent at the end of every round. Each of the following three lines contains three integers. Each line has a special attack’s base damage bb, extra damage ee, and cooldown cc. The 6th line is a positive integer, which is normal attack’s base damage.

  • R,H,b,c≥1R,H,b,c≥1
  • g,e≥0g,e≥0

Output Format

Output the health of the dragon after the damage and before adding gg at each round.
