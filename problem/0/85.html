85. Sort Dates

I'm a slow walker, but I never walk backwards.

  • Status
  • Statistics
  • Solution

Task Description

Write a program to sort dates. A date consists of year, month, and day. A date is smaller than another date if it comes before the other. For example, (1989,12,31)(1989,12,31) is smaller than (1990,1,1)(1990,1,1). Note that there are a large number of dates so using bubble sort will simply be a waste of time. You should use qsort to speed things up.

Input Format

Input file contains a integer nn (1≤n≤1,000,0001≤n≤1,000,000), which indicates the number of dates to be sort. And the following nn lines each contains three integers, YY, MM and DD, which represent the year, month and day of the date.

Output Format

Output the sorted dates line by line.

Sample Input

10
2009 4 9
2009 2 16
2010 4 1
2010 10 23
2009 9 1
2009 11 21
2009 10 12
2010 3 27
2009 1 4
2009 11 12

Sample Output

2009 1 4
2009 2 16
2009 4 9
2009 9 1
2009 10 12
2009 11 12
2009 11 21
2010 3 27
2010 4 1
2010 10 23
