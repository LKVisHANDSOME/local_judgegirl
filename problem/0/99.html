99. Bingo

I'm a slow walker, but I never walk backwards.

  • Status
  • Statistics
  • Solution

Task Description

Write a program to play bingo. A bingo board has mm rows and mm columns. Each entry has a different number from 11 to m×mm×m. The numbers from 11 to m×mm×m will be randomly called. If a player has all the numbers in a row, a column, or a diagonal called he can declare bingo and win the game. Now given the bingo boards of nn players, determine who wins the bingo.

Input

The first line of the input has nn and mm. Each of the next mm lines has the mm numbers of the bingo board of the first player. The next line has the name of the second player, and the next mm lines have the numbers for the second player, and so on. The next line has m×mm×m numbers that are called in sequence during the bingo game.

Output

Print the indices of the winners of the game and by which number they won the game. If there are more than one winner, print the player indices one by one according to the order they appear in input.

Limits

  • nn positive is no more than 1010.
  • mm is positive and no more than 256256.
  • The length of a name is positive and no more than 64. The name consist of only letters.

Sample input

2 3
Alice
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9
Blob
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9
1 2 4 8 6 3 9 5 7

Sample output

3 Alice Blob
